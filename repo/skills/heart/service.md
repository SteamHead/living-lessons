# Service

Install or implement your project in a real world location.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Have an execution plan for your project in the real world.

**2 — Early.** Implement your project in your immediate environment (e.g. classroom).

**3 — Preparatory.** Implement your project in your community (e.g. school, home).

**4 — Intermediate.** Implement your project in your city (e.g. park, mall).

**5 — Advanced.** Implement your project in your world (e.g. a school or park in another region).
