# Field Research

Visit real world locations and record data on your users.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Visit real-world locations and record data on a template.

**4 — Intermediate.** Select and identify field locations, visit them, and record self-identified data on them.

**5 — Advanced.** Select, identify, and visit locations. Take photos, record data, and create a location map.
