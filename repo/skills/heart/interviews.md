# Interviews

Interview people to understand who they are, what they do, why, and in what environment.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Interview your classmates and parents verbally, and draw the answers.

**2 — Early.** Interview your classmates and parents verbally from a visual template, and draw the answers.

**3 — Preparatory.** Create an interview guide, interview others, and draw and write the answers.

**4 — Intermediate.** Write a detailed interview guide, interview others, and write the results.

**5 — Advanced.** Write a detailed interview guide, interview others, and record the results (written, audio, video).
