# Brainstorm

Discuss and record 5-10 ideas for designs, from typical to wild.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Individually or in loose groups, draw a multitude of pictures of things that could help your subject.

**2 — Early.** Individually or in loose groups, draw lots of pictures and add text labels of anything helping your subject, including non-traditional ideas.

**3 — Preparatory.** Draw and write a multitude of solutions, including outside-of-the-box ideas, in a group of at least 3, taking turns recording. The group reviews the final list together.

**4 — Intermediate.** Draw and write lots of things that help your subject, including non-traditional ideas. Work in groups of 3, then combine into larger groups (6 or 9) to share ideas and continue.

**5 — Advanced.** Identify a brainstorm process (online, interviews, or other sources) and have your group follow it. Record your results, and reflect on the process's usefulness.
