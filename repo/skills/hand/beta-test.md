# Beta Test

Use your prototype in a controlled situation, but with real users and environments.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Use your prototype in a controlled situation, but with real users and environments. Talk about what happened, why, and whether your prototype worked as expected.

**4 — Intermediate.** Use your prototype in a controlled situation with real users and environments. Make a video of the test, and present the results.

**5 — Advanced.** Test your prototype in a controlled situation, record the results, and write an analysis of the results.
