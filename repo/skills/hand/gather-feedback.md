# Gather Feedback

Get constructive feedback from others, via a presentation, sharing experience, or series of demonstrations.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Show one piece of your work to a classmate, and talk about it together.

**2 — Early.** Ask your classmates to help you complete a delta/plus activity, with a piece of your work as the subject.

**3 — Preparatory.** Get constructive feedback from others, via a presentation, sharing experience, or a series of demonstrations.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Run a structured feedback activity (delta/plus, gallery walk) on your work, record the feedback you receive, and choose which suggestions to act on.

**5 — Advanced.** Create a feedback session, including a presentation of your work, a survey of responses, and an analysis of the results.
