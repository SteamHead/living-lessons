# Reflection

To better understand others, reflect upon your own purpose, biases, viewpoint, and goals.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Talk about some things you would like to do in your project, and if/how those things can help people.

**3 — Preparatory.** List reasons you want to complete your project, write them as goals, and note what you will need to learn to achieve those goals.

**4 — Intermediate.** To better understand others, reflect upon your own purpose, biases, viewpoint, and goals in an essay.

**5 — Advanced.** For each of your steps during a project, create a small self-reflection entry in a design journal.
