# Setup and Cleanup

Prepare your workspace and materials, and leave everything better than you found it.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** As a class, each student retrieves and cleans up some of the project materials.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Retrieve and clean up your own project materials each session, and check your work area before you leave.

**3 — Preparatory.** Work in a group; assign material retrieval and cleanup roles for each member.

**4 — Intermediate.** Independently prepare for your work, including materials and plans, then independently pack away everything.

**5 — Advanced.** Independently prepare for your work and pack away everything, including a team meeting on session goals accomplished and objectives for the next session.
