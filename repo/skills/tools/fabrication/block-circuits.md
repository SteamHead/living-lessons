# Block Circuits

Build a guided project, open project, and solo invention using littleBits or similar.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Equipment: littleBits or similar. Projects: simple guided project, then free experimentation time with more guided projects available.

**3 — Preparatory.** Equipment: littleBits or similar, plus adhesives and paper to enhance form. Projects: guided project, then design challenge.

**4 — Intermediate.** Equipment: Grove Zero or similar, plus adhesives and paper to enhance form. Projects: guided project, free play with a project library available, then design challenge.

**5 — Advanced.** Equipment: Lego Mindstorms, MakeBlock, or similar. Projects: guided project (commonly about 3 hours), then design challenge.


## Notes

Formerly named **Construction Kits** — that older ladder (Lego bricks, linear blocks, MakeBlock, custom kits) is retired in favor of this one.
