# Stitch Craft

Measure, outline, cut, sew, dye and print onto fabric to create softgoods — by hand.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Equipment: felt, adhesives, needle and thread, scissors, templates, stuffing. Projects: single-stitch-line projects like pillows, monster dolls, or capes.

**3 — Preparatory.** Equipment: prior + cotton or polyester. Projects: wearable or made-to-fit projects like hats, bags with straps, dust covers, bracelets.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Equipment: prior + embroidery hoop and multiple stitch types (running, back, whip). Projects: multi-piece hand-sewn projects from templates, like stuffed animals or pouches with closures.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Equipment: prior + student-chosen fabrics and notions. Projects: custom-designed, multi-piece hand-sewn projects with decorative stitching (embroidery, applique) from the student's own pattern.
