# Electro Craft

Solder wires, use heat guns, and power supplies while making projects.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Create a breadboard model of your concept, and roleplay what its usage would be like.

**4 — Intermediate.** Create a prototype of the electrical components of your project. It doesn't have to perform as designed, only demonstrate the basic concepts.

**5 — Advanced.** Solder components and wires together, build a case for your product, and install it in a real environment.
