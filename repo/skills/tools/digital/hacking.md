# Hacking

Download and modify programs or media.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Physical computing: find an object in your environment and modify it to improve it or change its purpose.

**2 — Early.** Download and modify pictures and drawings. Learn about fair-use policies worldwide.

**3 — Preparatory.** Download and modify simple programs (usually games). Review licenses, re-upload, choose an open-source license, and share.

**4 — Intermediate.** Download and modify hardware (often 3D models or laser-cut models). Review licenses, and re-share your changes.

**5 — Advanced.** Make a significant contribution to an open-source program or hardware design.
