# Account Creation

Create and responsibly manage online accounts and credentials.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Create an account on a school-approved website; record the URL, username, and password. Show you can retain and use this information over several days or weeks.

**4 — Intermediate.** Manage multiple accounts on different websites, and successfully perform a password reset.

**5 — Advanced.** Create and manage your own email address, password manager, and multiple website accounts, on both computers and mobile devices.
