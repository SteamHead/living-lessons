# Media Explorer

Read, watch, and listen to media about makers and topics to spark your own ideas.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Watch or read a maker story chosen by the teacher — a build video, a picture book, an article. Say what the maker made and why it matters.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Choose a maker story from the class collection. Record one idea from it you would like to try, and tell a partner what caught your attention.

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Find media about your project topic in the school library or on approved sites. Save what you find into your folder with a clear name.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Collect at least three sources about a topic in different formats — video, article, pictures. Compare them: which inspired you most, and what did each add?

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Follow a maker, topic, or channel over time outside class. Bring back regular finds to your team, and explain how they could shape your project.
