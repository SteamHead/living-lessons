# Empowered Researcher

Find the history of your audience to understand their culture and environment.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Self-select books from a cart on the culture or history of your interview subjects.

**3 — Preparatory.** Select books from a physical or digital library on your subject's culture and environment.

**4 — Intermediate.** Search a digital library for your subject's history.

**5 — Advanced.** Search online for your subject's present and historical culture and environment.
