# Empowered Researcher

Find the history of your audience to understand their culture and environment.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Look through picture books from the class cart about your subject. Find one picture that teaches you something about their life, and share it with a partner.

**2 — Early.** Self-select books from a cart about the culture or history of your interview subjects. Find two facts that help you understand their world better.

**3 — Preparatory.** Select books from a physical or digital library about your subject's culture and environment. Take notes on what shapes their daily life.

**4 — Intermediate.** Search a digital library for your subject's history. Collect what you find into your folder, and write a short summary of what their past explains about their present.

**5 — Advanced.** Search online for your subject's present and historical culture and environment. Combine several sources into a picture of their world, and cite where each fact came from.
