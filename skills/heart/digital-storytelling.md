# Digital Storytelling

Write an article and post a photo about your project.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Write an article with a photo into a document, and submit it electronically to the teacher.

**4 — Intermediate.** Write an article and post a photo about your project on an online school blog.

**5 — Advanced.** Write an article with a story, photos, video, and external citation links about your project on an online blog.
