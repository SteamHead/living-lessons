# Digital Storytelling

Write an article and post a photo about your project.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Tell your project's story out loud while showing one photo of it. Say what you made, who it is for, and one thing you are proud of.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Write two or three sentences about your project to go with a photo on the classroom wall or class page. Include what it does and who it helps.

**3 — Preparatory.** Write an article with a photo about your project and submit it electronically to your teacher. Tell the story: the problem, the build, and what happened.

**4 — Intermediate.** Write an article and post it with a photo on the school's online blog. Make sure the story makes sense to readers who never saw your project in person.

**5 — Advanced.** Publish an article on an online blog with a story, photos, video, and links to your sources. Craft it so a maker anywhere could learn from your project.
