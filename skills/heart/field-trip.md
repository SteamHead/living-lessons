# Field Trip

Attend an event, exhibition, or presentation as an audience member and collect inspiration.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Attend a presentation, exhibition, or demonstration with your class — like another class showing their cardboard games. Afterwards, say one thing that inspired you and why.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Attend an event with a looking-for list. Draw or write about two projects you liked, and note one idea you might borrow for your own making.

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* At an event or exhibition, ask a presenter a question about how they made their project. Record their answer and share it with your team.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Document a visit with photos or notes: what people made, how it worked, what visitors did. Present your top finds to your team and suggest one idea to try.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Attend a maker event outside school — a maker faire, a museum, another school's exhibition — and bring back what you discovered. Present it to the class as inspiration.
