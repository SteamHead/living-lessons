# Mood Board

Collect and arrange images and drawings that capture your subject's environment, culture, and problems.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Draw several pages of your subjects' environment and problems. No storylines, just single instances.

**4 — Intermediate.** Create a multi-page binder of drawings and cut-out pictures of your subjects and their environment. Name some problems they experience.

**5 — Advanced.** Create a wall-sized mood board showing the problems, environment, and culture of your subjects.
