# Mood Board

Collect and arrange images and drawings that capture your subject's environment, culture, and problems.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* As a class, collect pictures that remind you of your subject onto one big poster. Talk about what the pictures show about their world.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* With a partner, cut out and glue pictures and drawings of your subject's world — their places, tools, and daily life — onto a shared page.

**3 — Preparatory.** Draw several pages of your subjects' environment and problems — single moments, not storylines. Choose the drawing that best shows what their world feels like.

**4 — Intermediate.** Create a multi-page binder of drawings and cut-out pictures of your subjects and their environment. Name some problems they experience, and mark where each appears.

**5 — Advanced.** Create a wall-sized mood board showing the problems, environment, and culture of your subjects. Present it, and let it spark your team's problem-finding.
