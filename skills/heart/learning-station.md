# Learning Station

Do an activity just to learn it — even if it never joins your project.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Join a teacher-led learning station with your class and try the activity — like practicing glue-path control at the hot glue station. Say one thing you learned or want to try again.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* With a partner, complete a learning station by following its instruction card from start to finish. Take turns, help each other, and leave the station ready for the next makers.

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Choose a learning station yourself, follow its instructions independently, and finish the activity. Leave the station better than you found it, and tell a teammate what it taught you.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Complete a learning station outside your comfort zone — a tool or topic you have never tried. Record what you learned and what surprised you in your design journal.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Find a workshop or tutorial outside class — a video, a library program, a makerspace event — and try it. Bring what you learned back and show your team.
