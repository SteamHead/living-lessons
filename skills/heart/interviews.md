# Interviews

Interview people to understand who they are, what they do, why, and in what environment.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Interview classmates and family members by asking questions out loud, and draw their answers. Practice listening all the way to the end before you start drawing.

**2 — Early.** Interview people using a visual template that shows what to ask, and draw their answers in the boxes. Compare drawings with a partner: what did you each learn?

**3 — Preparatory.** Create your own interview guide with questions you choose. Interview others, and record their answers in drawings and writing. Circle the most surprising answer.

**4 — Intermediate.** Write a detailed interview guide, interview several people, and write up the results. Look across the interviews: what problems or wishes did more than one person mention?

**5 — Advanced.** Write a detailed interview guide and record full results — written, audio, or video. Interview people beyond your classroom, and summarize the patterns you found for your team.
