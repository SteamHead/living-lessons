# Field Research

Visit real world locations and record data on your users.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* As a class, walk a location in your school — the lunchroom, the playground — and record what you see on a picture template. Notice what people do there.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* With your group, observe one location in school and record simple data: how many people, what they use, what problems you spot. Share your notes with the class.

**3 — Preparatory.** Visit real-world locations and record data on a template: what you see, hear, and notice about how people use the space.

**4 — Intermediate.** Select field locations yourself, visit them, and decide what data matters. Record your self-identified data, and explain to your team why you chose to track it.

**5 — Advanced.** Select, identify, and visit locations — including places beyond school, with family or on a trip. Take photos, record data, and create a location map of what you found.
