# Service

Install or implement your project in a real world location.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Make an execution plan for your project in the real world: where it will go, who will use it, what you need, and the steps to get it there.

**2 — Early.** Implement your project in your immediate environment, like your classroom. Watch how people use it, and note one thing that works and one thing to improve.

**3 — Preparatory.** Implement your project in your community — your school or home. Get permission from the people in charge, install it, and check on it after a week.

**4 — Intermediate.** Implement your project in your city, like a park or a mall. Work with adults to arrange the location, install it properly, and gather reactions from real users.

**5 — Advanced.** Implement your project in the world — a school, park, or partner space in another region. Coordinate with people far away, and document the journey for others to follow.
