# Driving Question

Turn what you learned into a "How might we..." question that guides the whole project.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* As a class, turn a problem you found into a question that starts with 'How might we...'. Try saying it different ways until it sounds exciting to answer.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* With a partner, write your own 'How might we...' question about a problem from your research. Check: could there be lots of different answers to it?

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Write a driving question that names who you are helping and what would change for them — like 'How might we help the janitor move heavy bins safely?'

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Write several driving questions for the same problem — broader and narrower versions — and pick the one that is the right size for your project. Explain your choice.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Write and refine a driving question with your team, check it against your research, and revisit it mid-project to see if it still fits what you are building.
