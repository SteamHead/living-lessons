# User Stories

Write or record stories of people having problems that you want to address, using an AGILE format.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Draw a problem that your subjects experience.

**2 — Early.** Draw and add simple descriptions to a problem that your subjects experience.

**3 — Preparatory.** Write a story script of problems that your subjects experience; optionally add drawings.

**4 — Intermediate.** Write stories of people having problems that you want to address, using an AGILE format.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Write AGILE-format stories for several different users, prioritize them together as a team, and check them against your real interview and research notes.
