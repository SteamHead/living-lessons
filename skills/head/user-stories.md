# User Stories

Write or record stories of people having problems that you want to address, using an AGILE format.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Draw a problem that your subjects experience. Show who is in the picture, where they are, and what is going wrong for them.

**2 — Early.** Draw a problem your subjects experience and add simple descriptions: who it happens to, when it happens, and how it makes them feel.

**3 — Preparatory.** Write a story script of problems your subjects experience, with drawings if you like. Read it aloud — does it sound like something that really happens?

**4 — Intermediate.** Write stories of people having problems you want to address, using an AGILE format: as a ___, I want ___, so that ___.

**5 — Advanced.** Write AGILE-format stories for several different users, prioritize them together as a team, and check each one against your research — cut any story your notes can't support.
