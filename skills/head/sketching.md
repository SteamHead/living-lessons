# Sketching

Drawing rough drafts of solutions you think of; only good enough to convey general meanings.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Draw out a solution you'd like to build.

**2 — Early.** Draw out a solution you'd like to build, listing out the materials needed.

**3 — Preparatory.** Create a technical drawing of your planned solution including material labels and descriptions of function.

**4 — Intermediate.** Create a technical drawing of your planned solution, including measurements, materials, and descriptions of function.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Create a set of technical drawings showing your solution from multiple views, with measurements, materials, and functions clear enough for someone else to build from.
