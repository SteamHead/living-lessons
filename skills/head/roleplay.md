# Roleplay

Act out your subjects' situations with simple props to uncover and define the problems they face.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Using simple props, roleplay experiencing a problem that the subject might have.

**3 — Preparatory.** Identify subject problems, and roleplay them in a group to better understand the issue.

**4 — Intermediate.** Roleplay subject problems, breaking them into phases. Name and discuss each phase.

**5 — Advanced.** Roleplay subject problems, record the play, and write out the problem definition from it.
