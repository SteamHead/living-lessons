# Work Plan

Plan your project: from design thinking boards to full project proposals.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** As a class, fill out a design stages board (inspire, empathize, define, ideate, prototype, test) with your templates and project pictures.

**2 — Early.** Each student organizes templates and project pictures into a Design Stages notebook.

**3 — Preparatory.** Organize your design stages notebook, and track a summary of your progress on a template.

**4 — Intermediate.** Create a design stages wall board and track your project.

**5 — Advanced.** Create a design stages physical and electronic board (inspire, empathize, define, ideate, prototype, test) and track your project.


## Additional track: Business Plan

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Write what your project needs: time, skills, resources. Use a simple classroom template.

**4 — Intermediate.** Write a project proposal for what you need: time, skills, resources. Use a classroom template.

**5 — Advanced.** Write a project proposal for what you need: time, skills, resources. Use a professional template that you find and the teacher approves.
