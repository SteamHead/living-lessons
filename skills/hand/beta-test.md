# Beta Test

Use your prototype in a controlled situation, but with real users and environments.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Try your prototype yourself, the way a real user would. Say one thing that worked and one thing that did not.

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Watch a classmate try your prototype without helping them. Note where they get stuck, surprised, or delighted.

**3 — Preparatory.** Use your prototype in a controlled situation with real users and environments. Talk about what happened, why, and whether it worked as you expected.

**4 — Intermediate.** Beta test your prototype with real users, record the test on video, and present the results: what worked, what broke, what surprised you.

**5 — Advanced.** Test your prototype in a controlled situation, record the results, and write an analysis: what the data says, and what you will change in the next version.
