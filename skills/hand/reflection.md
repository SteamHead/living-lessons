# Reflection

Look back at what you did and why — what worked, what didn't, and what you'll do differently next time.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Circle the face that shows how today's work went, and say why. Naming how it went is the first step of reflecting.

**2 — Early.** Talk about some things you would like to do in your project, and how those things could help people.

**3 — Preparatory.** List the reasons you want to complete your project, write them as goals, and note what you will need to learn to achieve them.

**4 — Intermediate.** To better understand others, reflect on your own purpose, biases, viewpoint, and goals in an essay. What do you bring to this project — and what might you be missing?

**5 — Advanced.** For each step of a project, write a small self-reflection entry in a design journal. Over time, read back and notice how your thinking changed.
