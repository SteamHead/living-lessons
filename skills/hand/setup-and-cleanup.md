# Setup and Cleanup

Prepare your workspace and materials, and leave everything better than you found it.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** As a class, each student retrieves and cleans up some of the project materials. Everyone carries something; nobody's mess is only theirs.

**2 — Early.** Retrieve and clean up your own project materials each session. Check your work area before you leave — better than you found it.

**3 — Preparatory.** Work in a group and assign material retrieval and cleanup roles to each member. Rotate the roles so everyone learns every job.

**4 — Intermediate.** Independently prepare for your work — materials, tools, and plans — then independently pack everything away, including checking the floor for scraps and tools.

**5 — Advanced.** Independently prepare and pack away everything, and run a short team meeting: what did we accomplish this session, and what are our objectives for next time?
