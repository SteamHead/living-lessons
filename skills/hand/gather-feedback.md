# Gather Feedback

Get constructive feedback from others, via a presentation, sharing experience, or series of demonstrations.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Show one piece of your work to a classmate and talk about it together. Listen more than you explain.

**2 — Early.** Ask classmates to complete a delta/plus activity on a piece of your work: what could change (delta) and what is already working (plus).

**3 — Preparatory.** Get constructive feedback from others through a presentation, a sharing experience, or a series of demonstrations. Write down what you hear.

**4 — Intermediate.** Run a structured feedback activity — delta/plus, gallery walk — on your work. Record the feedback, and choose which suggestions to act on and why.

**5 — Advanced.** Create a full feedback session: present your work, survey the responses, and analyze the results. Share what you will change because of what you learned.
