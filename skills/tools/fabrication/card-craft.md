# Card Craft

Manipulating cardboard, foam core, and card stock is a core prototyping skill! Start simple, and keep adding projects (forever).

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Fold, tear, and tape paper into simple 3D shapes, following along with the teacher. Discover what paper can do before adding any tools.

**2 — Early.** With scissors, tape, regular paper, and cutting templates, complete a guided project and then a design challenge.

**3 — Preparatory.** Add recycled media — cups, plates, bags — to your paper toolkit. Complete a guided project and then a design challenge with mixed materials.

**4 — Intermediate.** Add cardboard, hot glue, and cardboard knives. Complete a guided project, a design challenge, and then a custom design — remembering tool safety every time.

**5 — Advanced.** Add the laser cutter to your card craft. Complete a guided project, a design challenge, a custom design, and finally a custom kit that others can build from.
