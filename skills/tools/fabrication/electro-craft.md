# Electro Craft

Solder wires, use heat guns, and power supplies while making projects.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Watch a mentor solder and use a heat gun. Point out the hot zones, the safe holds, and where fingers never go.

**3 — Preparatory.** Create a breadboard model of your concept, and roleplay how it would be used in real life.

**4 — Intermediate.** Create a prototype of your project's electrical components. It does not have to perform as designed — only demonstrate the basic concepts.

**5 — Advanced.** Solder components and wires together, build a case for your product, and install it in a real environment where people will actually use it.
