# 3D Printer

Start off downloading premade design files and using the machine, later design your own!

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Download a premade 3D model, use the 3D printer with a mentor's assistance, retrieve the printed pieces, and re-tape the printer bed for the next student.

**4 — Intermediate.** Load a 3D model into the printer, activate it, retrieve a successful print, and prepare the printer for the next student. Troubleshoot issues.

**5 — Advanced.** Maintain and troubleshoot a 3D printer for several weeks. Write training instructions for 3D printer users in your school.
