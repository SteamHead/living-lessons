# Stitch Craft

Measure, outline, cut, sew, dye and print onto fabric to create softgoods — by hand.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *(AI draft — review)* Practice the stitching motion with lacing cards or a plastic needle and burlap. Get the rhythm: down, across, up, pull.

**2 — Early.** With felt, needle and thread, scissors, templates, and stuffing, make single-stitch-line projects like pillows, monster dolls, or capes.

**3 — Preparatory.** Add cotton or polyester fabrics. Make wearable or made-to-fit projects like hats, bags with straps, dust covers, or bracelets.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Add an embroidery hoop and multiple stitch types — running, back, whip. Hand-sew multi-piece projects from templates, like stuffed animals or pouches with closures.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Choose your own fabrics and notions. Design and hand-sew custom multi-piece projects with decorative stitching — embroidery, applique — from your own pattern.
