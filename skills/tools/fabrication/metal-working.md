# Metal Working

Welding, cutting, melting and manipulating metals!

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Explore soft metals safely: bend, twist, and shape wire or aluminum foil into a model, using pliers and eye protection.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* With one-on-one adult guidance, cut and file a soft metal piece (aluminum or copper) for a project, and smooth all the edges.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* With close supervision, complete a project that joins metal parts (rivets, bolts, or supervised soldering), and finish the surfaces safely.
