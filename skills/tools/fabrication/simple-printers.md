# Simple Printers

Give students control! Large scale, tiled, new media. Responsible and effective use of this tool is where to start.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** Create a poster using a simple program (Word, PowerPoint, Publisher). Print with a teacher's assistance, cut the margins, and assemble into a large-format poster.

**4 — Intermediate.** Use a printer for several projects independently. Print on different paper sizes and color settings, and research the cost of ink and paper.

**5 — Advanced.** Use a printer independently: install printer drivers, configure print settings, perform a multi-sheet print, cut the margins, and assemble a large poster.
