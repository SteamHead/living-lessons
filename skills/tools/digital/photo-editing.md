# Photo Editing

Edit photos to improve and communicate: crop, adjust, combine, and prepare images for posts, posters, and projects.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Take a photo and make simple edits on a tablet or phone: crop, rotate, and brightness.

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Edit photos for a project: crop for framing, adjust light and color, and save the edited copy into the right folder with a clear name.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Combine or retouch images (remove a distraction, add text or stickers) for a poster or post, keeping the original file safe.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Plan and produce a set of edited photos with a consistent look for a real audience (exhibition, blog post), and explain your editing choices.
