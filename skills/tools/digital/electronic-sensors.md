# Electronic Sensors

Light, motion, and sound to start, more sensors possible.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Try out light, motion, and sound sensors in a plug-and-play kit, and describe what each one detects.

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Use one sensor to trigger an output (light turns on an LED, a clap makes a sound) in a guided project.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Combine two or more sensors in a project, and adjust their sensitivity so the project works reliably.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Choose sensors for your own design, test and calibrate them in the real environment, and record what you changed and why.
