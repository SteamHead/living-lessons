# IoT App Design

Design a game or application that sends and receives data over the internet.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Send a message or signal between two classroom devices (micro:bit radio or similar), and describe how the data travels.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Build a guided project where a device sends data over a network to a screen or another device (e.g. temperature to a dashboard).

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Design and build your own connected app or device that sends and receives data over the internet, and test it from two different locations.
