# Robotics Coding

Use sensors and actuators to create cars, robots, machines.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Physical computing: act out the sequence of a program with your body — inputs, decisions, outputs — during physical-computing lessons.

**3 — Preparatory.** Use micro:bit or similar to light LEDs, play sounds, and accept button or motion input. Complete a guided project and install it somewhere real — a door, a glove, a game.

**4 — Intermediate.** Use block programming with motors and LEDs as outputs and external sensors as inputs — magnetic, light, touch. Complete a multi-hour guided project, then a design challenge.

**5 — Advanced.** Use S4A, Arduino, or Python for micro:bit with motors, LEDs, and external sensors. Complete a multi-hour guided project, then a design challenge of your own.
