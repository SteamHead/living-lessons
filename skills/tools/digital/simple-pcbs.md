# Simple PCBs

Voice recorder beatbox, electronic synthesizer, BIY Arduino board.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** *(AI draft — review)* Assemble a snap-together or pre-printed circuit kit (voice recorder, beatbox) by following the pictures, and make it work.

**3 — Preparatory.** *(AI draft — review)* Assemble a simple PCB kit by matching labeled components to the board, with an adult checking before power-on.

**4 — Intermediate.** *(AI draft — review)* Solder a beginner PCB kit at a supervised station and test it. Identify each component's job.

**5 — Advanced.** *(AI draft — review)* Build a build-it-yourself microcontroller board (BIY Arduino style), or repair a kit that doesn't work, explaining your troubleshooting.
