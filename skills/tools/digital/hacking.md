# Hacking

Download and modify programs or media.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** Physical hacking: find an object in your environment and modify it to improve it or change its purpose. Show the class the before and after.

**2 — Early.** Download and modify pictures and drawings. Learn about fair use around the world: what you may borrow, and how to credit the original maker.

**3 — Preparatory.** Download and modify simple programs — usually games. Review the license, make your changes, choose an open-source license, and share your version back.

**4 — Intermediate.** Download and modify hardware designs — 3D models or laser-cut files. Review the licenses, make improvements, and re-share your changes for other makers.

**5 — Advanced.** Make a significant contribution to a real open-source program or hardware design — a fix or feature the project's community actually accepts.
