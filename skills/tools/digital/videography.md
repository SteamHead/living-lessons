# Videography

Shoot a video to communicate something creatively.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Using phones or tablets, record — and re-record — a single-take video introducing a subject to the audience. Steady hands, clear voice, one take.

**3 — Preparatory.** Add a simple video editor. Record several scenes, edit them into a sequence, and add music and text.

**4 — Intermediate.** Add microphones. In a planned story, record several scenes, edit them into a sequence, and add a title, credits, and a call to action.

**5 — Advanced.** Add lighting, camera holders, and an advanced editor. Produce a planned story with title, credits, and a call to action — polished enough to publish.
