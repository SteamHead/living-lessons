# Videography

Shoot a video to communicate something creatively.

## Depth levels

**1 — Introductory.** *Not introduced at this level.*

**2 — Early.** Equipment: phones or tablets. Projects: record and re-record a single-take video introducing a subject to the audience.

**3 — Preparatory.** Equipment: phones or tablets plus a simple video editor. Projects: record several scenes, edit them into a sequence, add music and text.

**4 — Intermediate.** Equipment: prior + microphones. Projects: in a planned story, record several scenes, edit into a sequence, add a title, credits, and a call to action.

**5 — Advanced.** Equipment: prior + lighting, camera holders, and an advanced video editor. Projects: in a planned story, record several scenes, edit into a sequence, add a title, credits, and a call to action.
